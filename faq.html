General info

Taguette is a free and open source qualitative analysis tool. In Taguette, users can import their textual materials (in a variety of formats), highlight portions of text, and assign those highlights a tag. If self-hosting or using app.taguette.org, users can do this collaboratively with others. Users can also export all data they import into Taguette, in a variety of formats.

app.taguette.org Info

It is a version of Taguette hosted on our servers for free use by anyone! We host this from a rented dedicated server from OVH, and our server's location is in France.

To use app.taguette.org, you need to register with a username and a password. You can provide an email address optionally, which we only use in case you forget your password. We will never sell your information to anyone else, and we will only use it log you into your account.

TL;DR: do not upload sensitive data (e.g. patient information, student records) to our hosted version of Taguette.

A good reference for what data could be safely uploaded into app.taguette.org is the U.S. Health and Human Services Instituional Review Board exemption decision chart -- if the data is exempt under this chart, it's most likely OK to upload to our hosted version of Taguette. If you're in a country outside the U.S., please refer to the ethics or human subject research boards of your country for exact information.

If you need to work collaboratively on sensitive data, then we would recommend reaching out to information technology at your organization and asking about a secure server in which they could install Taguette. If you are an independent researcher or do not have an IT unit at your institution that can do this for you, we would recommend procuring a secure server and installing Taguette there using our self-hosting guide. Taguette can run well in many cloud hosting providers free tiers (depending on the size of your data), and many of these providers have addendums to cover sensitive data (which may or may not incur a cost).

Yes, we back up app.taguette.org daily and keep those backups for a year. We send backups to a bucket in Amazon Web Services, in encrypted form only.
